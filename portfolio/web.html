Surah Al-Mujadila, Ayah 11: "يَرْفَعِ ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ مِنكُمْ وَٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ ٱلْعِلْمَ دَرَجَٰتٍۢ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌۭ"

Translation: "Allah will raise those who have believed among you and those who were given knowledge, by degrees. And Allah is Acquainted with what you do."
